# Week 0 — Setup & Fundamentals

This is the runway week: get your machine ready, learn the git loop, and get the lay of the land before any real work starts.

**Pick the guide that matches your computer.** The steps differ by operating system, so follow the right one — don't mix them.

    <p>For any MacBook or iMac. Homebrew, Ruby, Rails, Postgres, git — all native, no extra layers.</p>
  </a>

  <a class="card" href="{{ '/week0_setup_and_fundamentals.html' | relative_url }}">

Both guides end the same way: Neta running on your machine, your project board copied, and your first pull request merged. Once you've hit the checklist at the bottom of your guide, you're ready for week one.
